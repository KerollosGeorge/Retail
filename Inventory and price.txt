first if product exists, then update avaliable Status of product to be true
then add the quantity of the product to the inventory
second according to the price equation 
and if there is a discount provided in the request body (the discount End Date should be greater than the current date)
then calculate the discount price and update the product price
price = unitPrice + (unitPrice * VAT/100) - discountPrice
